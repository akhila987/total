VIRAT KOHLI

He was born in Delhi, India on November 5, 1988.Virat is the first player in ICC cricket history to win all 3 ICC awards in a single year-ICC , ODI player of the year, ICC test player of the year and ICC player of the year award in 2018 . He is the rated as one of the best batsmen in the world. His father ,Prem Nath Kohli, worked as a criminal lawyer, and his mother, Saroj Kohli, served as a housewife. He has an older brother, Vikas, and an older sister,Bhawna.
